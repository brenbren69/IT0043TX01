Yatogami

100% God

Yato (夜やトと Yato?), also known by his given name, Yaboku (夜や卜ぼく Yaboku?), is one of the main three protagonists of Noragami. He is an obscure god who aims to become a God of Fortune.[1] In the past, he was known as a God of Calamity.

Yukine

100% Regalia

Yukine (雪ゆき音ね Yukine?), whose real name is Haruki Tajima (田た嶋じま春はる樹き Tajima Haruki?),[5] is one of the three main protagonists of the ノラガミ Noragami series. He is Yato's shinki and guidepost, and a shinki of Father under the name Hagusa

Hiyori

50% Human, 50% Spirit

Hiyori Iki (壱い岐きひより Iki Hiyori?) is the main female protagonist of the ノラガミ Noragami series. She is a half-ayakashi (半はん妖よう hanyou?) whose spirit can temporarily leave her human body.

Bishamon-ten

100% God

Bishamonten (毘び沙しゃ門もん天てん Bishamonten?), also known as Vaisravana or Bishamon, is one of the Seven Gods of Fortune. She is officially a goddess of war and warriors.

Kazuma

100% Regalia

Kazuma (兆かず麻ま Kazuma?), whose real name is Kiyotsugu Hirano (平ひら野の清きよ次つぐ Hirano Kiyotsugu?), is Bishamonten's shinki and guidepost. He is also a shinki of Yato under the name Kazune
